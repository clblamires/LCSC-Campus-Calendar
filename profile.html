Degrees/Honors Received:

Bachelor of Arts in English Literature, The University of Montana, 1999; Bachelor of Arts in History, The University of Montana, 1999; Master of Public Administration, University of Idaho, 2009

Job Description:

Celeste is a member of the Administrative Computing team within IT. The team supports Datatel Colleague, the college's enterprise information system for student and financial data, and its web interface WarriorWeb. Celeste performs applications programming and other work related to maintaining Colleague's database, hardware and operating systems

Bio:

Celeste came to LCSC in 2005 from Seattle University where she worked as a Programmer/Analyst and had begun pursuing a Masters in Public Administration. She has completed her MPA at the University of Idaho, and is exploring research opportunities related to a Ph.D. Celeste also volunteers as the Newsletter Editor for the Spina Bifida Association of Washington State.
