Get Involved Stay Connected

Connect with Fellow AU Alumni

Check out all the different ways that you can connect with alumni in your area and around the world. Between alumni events locally, domestically, and internationally; an online presence including groups on Facebook and LinkedIn, and an active Twitter feed; benefits and discounts for alumni; educational programs; and a new and improved online directory, the question isn't if you can connect with AU, but how you'd like to do it!

10 Easy Ways to Stay Involved with AU

  1. Update your contact information to receive news and invitations.
  2. Attend regional, local, or international alumni events.
  3. Register as a member of the AU Alumni Association Online Community.
  4. Attend All-American Weekend: A Celebration for Alumni and Families, in October.
  5. Submit a Class Note.
  6. Display your Eagle pride at AU athletics events.
  7. Make a gift!
  8. Volunteer to assist with programming in your area.
  9. Post jobs or internships on AU's CareerWeb.
  10. Read Alumni Update, the monthly e-newsletter designed to keep you up to date on all of the latest news!
